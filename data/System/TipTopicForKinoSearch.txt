---+ Kino Search Operators

Kino Search allows you to use Google style operators to narrow down your search. For example, use the ==+== and ==-== operators to indicate _required_ and _forbidden_ terms, or use ==type:doc== to get all attachments of given type. For more information, see the [[%SYSTEMWEB%.KinoSearch][search help topic]].
